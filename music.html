2018 · Trio

Lantskap Logic

Evelyn Davis (pipe organ) · Fred Frith (electric guitar) · Phillip Greenlief (alto & tenor saxophones)

Recorded at the Mills College Chapel in Oakland — chosen for both its sound and its pipe organ — the trio's debut brings together three musicians whose work spans avant rock, free improvisation, and experimental composition. The intensity of listening in response to space and sonority; the reconciliation of elegance with free expression. Profane improvised music as beautiful as a sacred hymn.
